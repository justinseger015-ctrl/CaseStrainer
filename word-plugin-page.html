Easy Installation

Get started with CaseTrainer Word Plugin in minutes

1
Download the Plugin

Download the CaseTrainer Word Plugin manifest file from our secure server.

2
Add to Word

In Word, go to Insert > Add-ins > My Add-ins > Upload My Add-in and select the downloaded file.

3
Configure Settings

Set up your API key and preferences in the plugin settings panel.

4
Start Verifying

Click the CaseTrainer button in the Home tab to begin citation verification.

How to Use

Simple steps to verify citations in your legal documents

Basic Usage

  1. Open your legal document in Microsoft Word
  2. Click the CaseTrainer button in the Home tab
  3. Click "Scan Document for Citations"
  4. Review the verification results in the task pane
  5. Insert verified citations or highlight them in your document

Advanced Features

  • Selected Text Verification: Verify specific citations by selecting text and clicking "Verify Selected"
  • Auto-Highlighting: Enable automatic highlighting of verified citations in your document
  • Parallel Citations: Include parallel citations from multiple legal databases
  • Custom Formatting: Configure citation insertion format to match your style guide
