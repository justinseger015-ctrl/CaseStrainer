Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CaseTrainer Browser Extension

CaseTrainer Browser Extension supports Chrome 88+, Firefox 85+, and Microsoft Edge 88+. The extension uses Manifest V3 for modern browser compatibility and security.
The extension uses advanced pattern recognition and machine learning algorithms to detect legal citations in webpage text. It recognizes various citation formats including U.S. Supreme Court cases, federal court decisions, state cases, and statutory citations.
Yes, your privacy is protected. The extension only processes citation text and does not store your browsing history or personal data. All API calls are encrypted, and no permanent storage of your web activity occurs.
Yes, you can customize highlighting preferences in the extension settings. Choose your preferred colors for verified and unverified citations, or disable highlighting entirely if preferred.
The extension verifies citations against multiple sources including CourtListener, web search engines, and local legal databases. This multi-source approach ensures comprehensive and accurate verification results.
